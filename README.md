# Privacy Policy for Top100

**Last Updated:** March 2026

Welcome to Top100. This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, and protects your data. By using the app, you agree to the data practices described in this directive.

## 1. Data Collection & Usage

This application utilizes Firebase services—including Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, and Crashlytics—to function effectively. As a result, certain technical data and usage metrics are collected automatically. 

This data includes:
* **Device Information:** IP address, device model, and operating system.
* **Usage Data:** Interaction metrics within the app to help us monitor stability and improve the user experience.

## 2. AI Processing Disclosures

We utilize external Artificial Intelligence providers (such as Google Gemini or OpenAI) to generate and process certain game elements, including dynamic content generation. 

* **Gameplay Data:** By using this app, you consent to the necessary gameplay data being transmitted to these AI providers solely for the operation of game features. 
* **Personally Identifiable Information (PII):** No personally identifiable information is sent to these third-party AI models unless explicitly provided by you during gameplay.

## 3. Account Deletion & Data Retention

You retain full control over your data. A permanent **"Delete Account"** button is available in your designated profile editor. 

Activating this feature will permanently obliterate the following from our active servers:
* Your user profile
* Game scores
* Friends list
* Backend authentication data

## 4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to our development team.
